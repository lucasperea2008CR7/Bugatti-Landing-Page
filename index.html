Our Models

Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is a mid-engine two-seater sports car developed and manufactured in Molsheim, France by Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S. as the successor to the Bugatti Veyron.

Divo

The Bugatti Divo is a mid-engine sports car developed and manufactured by Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S. It was named after French racing driver Albert Divo, who raced for Bugatti in the 1920s.

Our History

Bugatti was founded in 1909 in Molsheim, France by Italian-born Ettore Bugatti. The company was known for producing high-quality and high-performance automobiles, with the Bugatti Type 35 being one of the most successful racing cars of all time. In the 1930s, Bugatti also produced some of the most luxurious cars of the time, such as the Bugatti Royale. Today, Bugatti continues to produce exclusive, high-performance sports cars that push the boundaries of engineering and design.
